About Me

Since the first time I booted up my Nintendo Entertainment System at the age of 3, I've been incredibly passionate about games. My life has been driven toward the career of game design; I want to create experiences that give people the same feelings of joy and wonder as I first experienced playing Super Mario Brothers.

I am developing a skillset that combines the creativity of crafting gameplay with analytics and scripting languages. I am currently employed as a Game Designer for SGN, where I am focused on data-oriented systems design and content refinement for the game "Cookie Jam."

My passion for design lies in creating and refining visceral, satisfying mechanics and gameplay, and a constant endeavor to improve myself and learn new things.
